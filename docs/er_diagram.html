Entity Relationship Diagrams

This simplified ER diagram shows the high-level relationships between the primary concepts in CoreCDM. In general, the Encounter table is used along with Encounter_SID as the primary key. However, the Person_SID foreign key can also be used to connect information across encounters if needed.
